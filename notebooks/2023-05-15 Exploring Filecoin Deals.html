Exploring Filecoin Deals

Author

David Gasquez

Published

May 15, 2023

Storage deals are core to Filecoin. They are the main mechanism by which clients and storage providers interact.

Let’s explore the data!

Data Sources

Deals data is available from the on-chain state (via the StateMarketDeals JSON-RPC method). The state doesn’t reflect the historical deals, but should be enough to get a sense of things.

Alternatively you can use an oracle like fil-naive-marketwatch, rely on APIs like Spacescope for aggregated data or reconstruct the deals from Lily CSV dumps, which can provide all deals in Filecoin history.

I’ll rely on Glif nodes export of StateMarketDeals on S3 (direct link).
